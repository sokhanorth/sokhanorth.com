The meaning behind the name

Sokha
សុខ
Khmer · ភាសាខ្មែរ

In Khmer, “Sokha” (សុខ) means health, happiness, and peaceful wellbeing. It is used as a greeting — “How is your Sokha?” — and reflects a state of being at ease, free from illness or hardship. At its root, it carries the wish for communities to be well, not merely to endure.
